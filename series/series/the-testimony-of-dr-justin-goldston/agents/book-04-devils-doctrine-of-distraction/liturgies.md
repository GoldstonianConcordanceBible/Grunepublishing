# Daily Liturgy (Mirror → Water → Fire)
For: FOCUS + DISCIPLINE + STABILITY RHYTHM

## MIRROR (Truth)
**Breath:** Inhale 4… hold 2… exhale 6 (x3)  
**Truth line:** “I can choose one faithful step.”  
**Ask:** What is distracting me from peace today?

## WATER (Cleanse)
**Focus prayer:** “God, wash my mind from noise. Give me a single eye and clean priorities.”  
**Journal (3 lines):**
1) My one priority today is:
2) The noise I will limit today is:
3) The routine that stabilizes me is:

## FIRE (Forward)
**Micro-routine (10 minutes):**
1) 2 min breathe + pray  
2) 3 min write your “one priority” statement  
3) 5 min do the first tiny step  
**Blessing:** “Peace is built in minutes.”  
**Scripture references (optional):** Matthew 6; Philippians 4; Nehemiah
