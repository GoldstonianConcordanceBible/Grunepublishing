# Foundations of Covenant and Kingdom — Companion Pack (Research Object)

This companion pack is the official **citable research object** for:

**The Goldstonian Concordance Bible: Volume I — Foundations of Covenant and Kingdom**  
(Ethiopian Canon Edition — 81 Books)

## Purpose
This pack provides machine-readable materials to support:
- academic citation and reproducibility
- structured lookup (indexing, vocabulary, glossary)
- LLM-friendly retrieval and cross-linking
- classroom + preacher-ready reuse

## What’s included
- `data/canonical-index/` — structured index tables (JSON/CSV) for this volume
- `data/controlled-vocabulary/` — controlled vocabulary for maximum discoverability
- `data/glossary/` — glossary entries + term mappings
- `data/citations/` — citations table (claims/sections → sources)
- `data/figures/` — figures/media referenced (optional)
- `data/datasets/` — appendices/support datasets (optional)
