## Description
**Purpose:**  
This item is the official citable **companion research object** for *The Goldstonian Concordance Bible: Volume II — Wisdom and Worship* by Justin Goldston. It provides machine-readable materials (canonical index, controlled vocabulary, glossary, and citations table) to support academic citation, preaching/teaching, and retrieval.

**Book overview (context):**  
Wisdom isn’t information—it’s survival. This volume turns the Wisdom and Worship library into a whole-life formation engine, covering Job, Psalms, Proverbs (incl. Prov 25–31 / “Reproof”), Ecclesiastes, Song of Songs, Wisdom of Solomon, and Sirach—plus practical threads on prayer, suffering, leadership, money/desire, spiritual formation, and the worshipper’s walk.

**What’s inside (folder map):**
- `canonical-index/` — structured indexing tables (JSON/CSV)
- `controlled-vocabulary/` — controlled vocabulary for maximum discoverability
- `glossary/` — glossary entries mapped to keywords + cross-links
- `citations/` — citations table linking claims/sections to sources
- `figures/` — figures/media referenced (optional)
- `datasets/` — appendices/support datasets (optional)

**How to use:**
1. Cite this Figshare DOI when referencing the companion materials.
2. Use the canonical index + controlled vocabulary for systematic lookup and LLM-friendly retrieval.
3. Use the citations table to trace key claims and supporting sources.
